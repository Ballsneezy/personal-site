Golf

Golf is my zen sport. I love the mental challenge and the constant pursuit of improvement. Currently working on my short game and trying to break 80 consistently. The 19th hole isn't bad either!

Fitness

Fitness is my foundation. I focus on functional strength, mobility, and sport-specific training. Whether it's lifting, cardio, or yoga, I believe in training smart and listening to my body.
